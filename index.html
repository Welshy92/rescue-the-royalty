Rescue The Royalty!

The heir to the throne of Royaltopia has gone missing. Without them the whole of society may become destabalized! You have been assigned to go out into the wilds to search for them. Good luck hero!

Check out the instructions below!

Enemies Defeated: 0

Hero Deaths: 0

You will be facing off against 1 of 6 potential enemies (chosen at random). It is a fight to the death so you must make sure the enemies hitpoints (HP for short) hits 0 before yours does! You will take it in turns to fight, starting with your move first. During your turn you have 6 options to chose from. A physical attack known as "Sword Swing", an attack for each of the 4 elements and a healing spell that will give you back some HP. At the start of each fight you will be assigned 1 random weakness and 1 random resistance. Each enemy has a type of attack that they are reisitant to which will cause your damage for that attack to be cut in half. Some enemies can also nullify certain types of attacks which will cause it to do no damage. Each enemy also has 1 weakness that will cause you do deal double damage. See the chart and table below for more information on what element is good to use and some information on each potiential enemy you can encounter.

You can read these instructions again at any point by clicking on the options button and selecting instructions.
